City Latitude vs Humidity

This graph is observing the relationship between City Latitude and Humidity Percentages. It seems that higher latitudes - the northern hemisphere - has more diverse percentages of humidity. There is more of a range of temperature, whereas cities with lower latitudes - the southern hemisphere - contains mostly humidity above 60% and did not see humidity below 30%.
